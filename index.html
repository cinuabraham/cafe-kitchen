OUR CHEF PICKS

A burger is a popular sandwich made from a cooked ground meat patty, typically beef, placed inside a sliced bun. It is often garnished with various toppings and condiments, offering a flavorful and satisfying handheld meal.

Toast is sliced bread that has been browned by exposure to heat, usually through toasting in a toaster or oven. It is a common breakfast item and can be served with butter, jam, or other toppings.

A muffin is a small, round baked bread product that is often sweet and can come in a variety of flavors such as blueberry, chocolate chip, or banana. It typically has a soft and moist texture, making it a popular choice for breakfast

Pizza is a popular Italian dish consisting of a round, flat dough base topped with various ingredients such as tomato sauce, cheese, and often meats or vegetables, then baked in an oven. It is enjoyed worldwide and comes in styles.
